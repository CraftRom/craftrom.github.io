Magisk Module that will turn your Android phone into a OnePlus device by adding:
- Oxygen OS Launcher and Icons Pack;
- Oxygen OS Widget and Weather;

### Requirements
- Android 11

### Installation
1. Flash this module.
2. Reboot.
3. Enjoy!
